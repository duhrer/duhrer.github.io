Fluid Gamepad - "Sequencer"

Each gamepad controls its own sequence of pulses (vibrations).

Controls

The d-pad navigates the grid (sequence). The face buttons Bumpers control the BPM.

Button 0 (X on a Playstation controller) sets the strong and weak vibration value for the step to 100%.

Button 1 (circle on a Playstation controller) sets the strong and weak vibration value for the step to 0.

Button 2 (square on a Playstation controller) sets the strong vibration to 100% and the weak to 0%.

Button 3 (triangle on a Playstation controller) sets the strong vibration to 0% and the weak to 100%.
